HEART DISEASE PREDICTION

World Health Organisation has estimated 12 million deaths occur worldwide,every year due to Heart diseases.The early prognosis of cardiovascular diseases can aid in making decisions on lifestyle changes in high risk patients and in turn reduce the complications.This project intends to pinpoint the most relevant/risk factors of heart disease as well as predict the overall risk using logistic regression.
